Get the best of coffee at Hot Shot Café

Hello, we’re Hot Shot Café, your new premium coffee service.
We know you're always craving a good cup of coffee, so let us take care of that — we’re really good at it! We use the highest quality arabica coffee as the base for our espresso drinks.

Open 24/7

Never worry about fresh coffee again! We work every day of the week, all year round. You can have you coffee to go, or drink it with us at one of our super cozy locations. We also have free wi-fi at the highest speeds.

Ready in 5 minutes

We work with the best baristas to ensure that you're 100% happy. Cappuccino, americano, espresso, macchiato, mocha, latte — you're only five minutes away from your delicious and super fresh cup of coffee.

100% organic

All our coffee is fresh and organic. Each bean is hand-picked and carefully selected from our own coffee suppliers in Colombia, Brazil, Ethiopia and beyond. We're delivering the flavour you want, without any waste.
